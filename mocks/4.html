Lesson 1: Greetings and Introductions

Lesson Content

This lesson introduces basic greetings and self-introductions in the language. You'll learn how to say hello, ask someone's name, and introduce yourself. Pay attention to the pronunciation and cultural context provided by the native speaker.

Interactive Exercises

Cultural Insights

Learn about the cultural significance of greetings in this community. Understand the nuances of formal and informal greetings, and the importance of respect in communication.
