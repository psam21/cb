How Culture Bridge Works

Culture Bridge provides the tools for communities to document, preserve, and share their unique cultural heritage with the world through decentralized technology.

Decentralized Network

Built on Nostr, ensuring content is censorship-resistant and globally accessible.

Community-Driven

Communities manage and curate their own cultural heritage content.

Secure Documentation

Utilizes secure, decentralized storage to protect cultural information.
